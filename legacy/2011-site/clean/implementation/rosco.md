::: {.field .field-type-text .field-field-description}
::: {.field-items}
::: {.field-item}
Rosco is an app that connects to an arduino over USB serial to convert
analog and digital signals into OSC messages. It employs an internal
quartz composer patch and broadcasts locally to an external patch or to
any other OSC client. It features 6 analog and 12 digital signals. It
also features a 8 x 16 button matrix that is currently only broadcasts
from the app and does not have a hardware equivalent.
:::
